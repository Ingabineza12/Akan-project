AKAN NAMES

The AKAN naming style is a way of naming in Ghana where the baby is named according to the day of the week he/she was born on.
with your Akan name you will know the day of the week you were born without concerning the calendar.
so, this will use your entered gender to know if you will be given a female or male name, and it will also use your born date to see the day you were born on.

Here is how names are depending on the day of the week:

DAY MALE FEMALE
Sunday Kwasi Akosua
Monday Kwadwo Adwoa
Tuesday Kwabena Abenaa
Wednesday Kwaku Akua
Thursday Yaw Yaa
Friday Kofi Afua
Saturday Kwame Ama
